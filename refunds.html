Refund Policy

This Refund Policy applies to all consulting, advisory, and implementation services provided by Online Distributions.

Consulting & Advisory Services

Due to the nature of consulting and advisory services — which involve time-based access, customized analysis, and strategic guidance — all payments are non-refundable once services have begun.

Distribution Diagnostic

The Distribution Diagnostic includes a scheduled 1:1 session and a customized assessment. Once the session has been delivered or the assessment work has started, the fee is non-refundable.

Build & Launch and Done-For-You Services

Project-based and implementation services involve upfront planning, allocation of resources, and reserved capacity. As a result, payments for these services are non-refundable once work has commenced.

Exceptional Circumstances

Refund requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis prior to service delivery. Any approved refund is issued at the sole discretion of Online Distributions.
